### Condition number

General definition:

$$
\kappa(A)=\lVert{A}\rVert\cdotp\lVert{A}\rVert^{-1}
$$

Symmetric positive definite:

$$
\kappa(A)=\frac{\max\lambda}{\min\lambda}
$$
